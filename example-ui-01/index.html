Work or school

I manage Google for my organization

Install Storx once for everyone in your company. Your team doesn’t need to sign up individually.

  • Back up whole teams at once
  • One place to see company-wide status
  • Helpful when someone leaves the company

You’ll need admin access to your company’s Google account.

Personal

It’s just my own account

Sign in with Google or Microsoft—the same way you log into your email. We only access what’s needed to back up your stuff.

  • Email, Drive/OneDrive, Calendar & Contacts
  • You stay in control of your data
  • Restore items yourself anytime

Takes about a minute.
